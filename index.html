Admission Process

  1. Online Registration: Fill out the online application form from our website.
  2. Merit/Entrance Based: Admission is based on merit in qualifying exams or scores from relevant entrance tests.
  3. Document Verification: Submit the required documents for verification at the college campus.
  4. Fee Payment: Complete the admission by paying the fees to confirm your seat.

Important Dates

  • Application Opens: June 1, 2025
  • Application Deadline: August 15, 2025
  • New Session Begins: September 1, 2025
